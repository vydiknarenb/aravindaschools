Dr.Chukka Ramaiah, an eminent academician and well known personality also known as IIT Ramaiah to every house in Andhra Pradesh, redefines the education standards with his exemplary skill.

Visionary by nature, he wants to improve the levels of learning at the primary & secondary. He founded the Society of promotion of Academic Excellence in Schools (SPAES) by bringing together like minded experienced teachers, academicians & schools.

SPAES pursues innovative programmes to provide deep conceptual learning to its students in its quest for excellence. Carrying forward the recognized legacy of providing best quality education, SPAES also serves as the only education body with a strong will to achieve excellence from school level itself.

At Aravinda we take great pains to implement the SPAES programme perfectly. We emphasize deep, conceptual understanding of concepts to take on the work to solve the challenging problems in the SPAES curriculum.

STEP (STUDENTS, TEACHERS, EDUCATORS & PARENTS)

One of the extraordinary activities in our school is dialogue which is carried out as a regular feature in assemblies with our friend philosopher and guide M.Shiva Ram. Shiva Ram sir is a noted educationist who founded a network organization called STEP

STEP is a network organization founded by M.Shiva Ram sir who is a friend, philosopher and guide to us. The aim of this organization is to develop through deep dialogue and training, students, teachers, educators & parents.

STEP has been active for a decade and has carried out hundreds of dialogue sessions and training programs.

STEP is head quartered at Aravinda and is close at hand to provide training and dialogue sessions to students, teachers, educators and parents.

STEP has many resource persons who have formed the network and they are all working under the guidance of M.Shiva Ram sir.

Quietly, yet powerfully STEP marches on to enlighten the world.

SAHES

SAHES ( Society for the Advancement of Holistic Education in Society) was started in the memory of Raja Sir to carry forward his vision of providing holistic education in the world.

The activities of SAHES are:

1. Developing books on all subjects on the school level that provides a holistic understanding of the world.

2. Providing an exposure to great books through book exhibition and sales, reading aloud sessions and discussions on great books.

3. Workshops on creative writing, public, speaking and indepth understanding of science.

4. Holding interactive sessions with great resource persons.

5. Conducting educational trips to famous places that facilitate learning.

6. Developing books written by children.
