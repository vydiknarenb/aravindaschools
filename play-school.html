Pre School

"MAGIC MOMENTS" - The Pre School

It is the universally accepted fact that early childhood is a stage when the child has an absorbent mind. This mind, this special stage of life endows child with almost a genius like capacity to take in things and take leaps in learning.

“Give me a child for the first 6 years and they can do whatever you want with him later”.

The above quote is absolutely true.

Hence the philosophy of our preschool is serious. We fill the moments spent in the preschool with enumerable activities that the child enjoys participating in. This leads to a tremendous growth and also interest in the school.

Thus the preschool also prepares him for the main school. It also gives him a security that Aravinda is a home away from home and he need not to fear on the contrary the child feels eager to come to school everyday knowing that he will find joy, meaning and fulfillment there.

“Magic Moments” is a place where life is truly magical!
